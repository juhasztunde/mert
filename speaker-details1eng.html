dr. Zachary P. Evans

Assistant Professor
Medical University of South Carolina (USA, SC)
College of Dental Medicine, Department of Stomatology

Zach Evans completed his PhD in Biomedical Science, dental training, and residency in periodontics at the Medical University of South Carolina, and is a Diplomate of the American Board of Periodontology.

Zach is currently an Assistant Professor in the MUSC Department of Stomatology, Division of Periodontics. He is director of the Master of Science in Dentistry program and is actively engaged in research relating to dental implant and CAD/CAM technologies. He serves as Chief Scientific Officer for CuRE Innovations, a biotech startup based in Charleston, developing antimicrobial additives and coatings for existing restorative and implant components and materials.

Zach lectures internationally on the incorporation of digital systems into surgical and implant practice.

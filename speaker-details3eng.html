Dr. Anthony Samuel Mennito

Anthony Mennito (Tony) is an Associate Professor at the Medical University of South Carolina College of Dental Medicine. He is the division director for digital dentistry and runs the CAD/CAM and Esthetic clinics at the school.

Tony was on the team that helped develop and test the E4D Compare computer assisted learning software and is the Chief Operations Officer for CuRE Innovations, a biotech startup that creates materials to improve restoration longevity. Tony lectures internationally and has numerous published papers on the topic of digital dentistry. He maintains a private practice in Charleston, South Carolina with an emphasis on CAD/CAM and esthetic restorations.
